Safety Information

⚠️ Important Safety Tips

  • Always store gas cylinders in a well-ventilated area
  • Check for leaks regularly using soapy water solution
  • Keep gas cylinders away from heat sources and flames
  • Ensure all connections are tight before use
  • Never use damaged or rusty cylinders
  • Turn off the gas valve when not in use
  • Keep children away from gas equipment
  • In case of leak, turn off supply and ventilate area immediately
