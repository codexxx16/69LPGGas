Safety Instructions

Storage Safety

  • Store gas cylinders in a well-ventilated area
  • Keep away from heat sources and direct sunlight
  • Store cylinders upright and secure them properly

Usage Precautions

  • Check for leaks regularly with soapy water
  • Never use a gas appliance if you smell gas
  • Ensure proper ventilation when using gas appliances

Emergency Procedures

  • In case of leak, turn off supply and evacuate area
  • Do not operate electrical switches if gas leak is suspected
  • Keep a fire extinguisher accessible near gas appliances
