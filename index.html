Safety Cautions

Home Safety

  • Always store gas cylinders upright in well-ventilated areas
  • Keep gas cylinders away from heat sources and direct sunlight
  • Never store gas cylinders indoors or in enclosed spaces
  • Check for gas leaks regularly using soapy water

Usage Safety

  • Always turn off the gas at the cylinder when not in use
  • Never use damaged or worn gas pipes and connections
  • Ensure proper ventilation when using gas appliances
  • Have your gas appliances serviced regularly

Emergency

  • If you smell gas, turn off the supply immediately
  • Ventilate the area and avoid using electrical switches
  • Contact emergency services if needed
